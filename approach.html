Strategy

The Revenue Architecture

Moving school districts beyond static ad sales and into sophisticated commercial rights management.

The Challenge

Bridging the Resource Gap

Texas high school athletic departments possess some of the most visible brands in their communities. However, the operational heavy lift of managing multimedia assets, digital rights, and regional sales often falls on over-extended staff already at capacity.

Our methodology is designed to eliminate this friction. We act as a professional, autonomous extension of your district, transforming passive commercial inventory into active, high-yield revenue streams.

Our Evolution

From Scoreboards to Strategy

MVP Marketing Group was founded by Michael Vogelaar after a decade inscoreboard equipment sales. He realized that districts weren't just looking for hardware—they were looking for sustainable strategies to maximize the long-term commercial value of those physical assets.

We transitioned from equipment providers to strategic advisors. Today, we manage the complete commercial lifecycle: from professional asset audit and 1:1 executive pitches to contract management, term stewardship, and high-satisfaction renewals.
