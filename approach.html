The Turnkey Methodology

Our Approach

Strategic Evolution

MVP Marketing Group was founded on a simple realization: high school districts are understaffed and over-extended. While they possess world-class athletic potential, they often lack the commercial sales infrastructure to maximize it.

We transitioned from hardware scoreboard sales to total revenue consulting to bridge this gap, providing a professionally managed, white-glove extension of your department.

Total Resource Management

Our approach saves your department time and energy while minimizing staff burnout. We handle the heavy lift—from creating custom marketing plans and identifying regional partners to managing final contract fulfillment.

By treating district sponsorships with the same level of care as professional sports franchises, we ensure long-term satisfaction for both the district and the sponsor.

Comprehensive Solutions

With over 30+ years of combined experience, we unlock revenue across three core commercial channels.

On-Site Visibility

Strategic static and digital signage at high-traffic complexes, stadiums, and gymnasiums. We optimize sightlines to ensure sponsor brands are inseparable from the game-day experience.

Digital Dominance

Integrating partners into the district's modern ecosystem: social media storytelling, website takeovers, and live-stream commercial placements that reach fans everywhere.

Multimedia Assets

Engaging the community through audio-visual synergy—public address announcements, video board featurettes, and custom halftime activations.

The Mission

MVP Marketing Group is dedicated to maximizing revenue for Texas high schools and collegiate properties. We bridge the gap between untapped potential and limited district resources through elite, turnkey sponsorship management.
