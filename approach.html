Strategic Evolution

MVP Marketing Group was born from a pivotal realization in the Texas high school market: districts were being sold hardware (scoreboards), but were being left without the commercial strategy to sustain them long-term.

Our founder, Michael Vogelaar, spent a decade in the equipment space before identifying the massive resource gap at the district level. Today, we act as an autonomous extension of your office, professionalizing your commercial assets to meet the standards of regional and national partners while removing the burden of sales from your staff.
