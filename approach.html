The MVP Approach

Strategic Evolution

Maximizing High School Potential

MVP Marketing Group is a growing consulting group here in Texas. We provide full-service sales, marketing solutions, and contract fulfillment offerings that aim to maximize revenues for various organizations and properties.

Our founder, Michael Vogelaar, spent a decade selling scoreboards. He realized that districts weren't just looking for hardware—they were looking for sustainable, professionally managed revenue streams. We transitioned from equipment sales to total revenue consulting to fill that gap.

Comprehensive Marketing Solutions

With over 30+ years of success in generating revenue for over 80 school districts, we provide the expertise needed to help our Clients with three core offerings:

On-Site Offerings

Static and digital signage at stadiums, gymnasiums, and complexes. We audit every sightline to ensure maximum partner visibility.

Digital Strategy

Leveraging district websites, social media channels, and live-stream broadcasts to reach fans where they are: on their screens.

Multimedia Integration

Audio announcements, video board features, and integrated sponsorship "activations" during game nights to engage the local community.

Revenue Fulfillment

We don't just sell. We manage the ad copy, the logo proofs, and the installation, so your staff can focus on the students.
