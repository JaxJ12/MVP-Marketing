Revenue Architecture

A Strategic Evolution

MVP Marketing Group was founded by Michael Vogelaar following a decade in scoreboard sales. He recognized that districts were being sold hardware, but lacked the long-term strategy to pay for it.

We pivoted from equipment sales to total commercial rights management. Today, we bridge the gap between world-class athletic potential and limited district resources.

The Burnout Solution

Athletic departments are over-extended. Our methodology is built to remove the burden of commercial sales entirely.
