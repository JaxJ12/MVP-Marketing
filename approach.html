The Challenge

Bridging the Resource Gap

High school athletic departments in Texas are some of the most visible brands in their communities. However, the heavy lift of managing multimedia assets, digital rights, and regional sales often falls on over-extended staff.

Our approach is designed to eliminate this friction. We act as a 100% autonomous extension of your district, transforming passive inventory into active, high-yield revenue streams.

Our Evolution

From Hardware to Strategy

Founded by Michael Vogelaar after a decade in scoreboard sales, MVP Marketing Group was built on the realization that districts don't just need equipment—they need sustainable revenue strategies.

We transitioned from equipment providers to strategic partners. Today, we manage the entire commercial lifecycle: from asset valuation and 1:1 executive pitches to final contract stewardship and year-over-year renewals.
