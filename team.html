Industry Leadership

Michael Vogelaar

CEO / Co-Founder

After a decade selling sponsorships with a leading scoreboard company, Michael founded MVP Marketing Group to provide a more comprehensive, strategic approach to district revenue. He has generated tens of millions in sponsorships across regional and national levels, securing multimillion-dollar naming rights for major school districts across Texas.

Michael’s expertise lies in identifying untapped asset value and building trust with local stakeholders to ensure long-term, high-yield partnerships.

Drew Mitchell

Co-Founder

Drew delivers turnkey solutions that maximize revenue and sponsorship satisfaction for organizations and properties across Texas. He brings extensive experience in strategic grassroots sports marketing, planning, and execution. Drew's focus is on the operational excellence that ensures every partnership is fulfilled to the highest standard.

His deep understanding of the Texas high school athletic landscape allows him to bridge the gap between district resources and professional commercial management.
